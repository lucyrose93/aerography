Dr Lucy Sabin

I am a British artist and researcher with a radically interdisciplinary background spanning geography, feminist science studies, and environmental justice. In my creative practice, I blend elements of graphic (web) design, participatory arts, installation design, art-science dialogue, and somatics. My recently awarded PhD Aerography (literally, to draw or write the air) is a critical and creative meditation on the concept of air quality. Now working as a postdoc with RE4GREEN at Amsterdam UMC, I'm researching environmental and climate justice in science and innovation, which involves producing a podcast and series of short films. I have held teaching posts in media, culture, and design at University of Sussex, the University for the Creative Arts, and Arts University Bournemouth.
